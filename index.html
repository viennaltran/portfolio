About Me

Hello, my name is Vienna Tran. I am a web developer based in Orange County. Growing up speaking three languages and my favorite school subject being art, I have always had an affinity for language and creativity.

My journey with web development began after I saw my friend completing a coding challenge. I was immediately hooked - coding perfectly combined my interest in seeking patterns in languages, and my fascination with using those patterns to make more complex forms that eventually turn into a beautiful piece. Coding is art, and after all the time and effort I put in, there are few things as profoundly rewarding as seeing my creations come to life.

Thank you for visiting my website. I hope to connect with you for exciting opportunities!

Latest Projects

Pocket Cards

A flash card generation app

Pocket Cards is a team project where we built a mobile-first web application that efficiently creates flashcards online for students studying for their next test or quiz.

Technologies used:

ReactJS, Redux, Materialize, NodeJS, MySQL, AWS

Grace Hopper

A tech events finder for women in tech

To help boost numbers of women in tech world, my team created an application targeted for women in tech to easily find tech events in OC and LA areas. Event information from meetup.com, locations from Google Maps, live tweets from Twitter, and images from Flickr are provided for users to see and use.

Technologies used:

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX, API's (Google Maps, Flickr, Twitter, and Meetup)

Student Grade Table

Technologies used:

HTML5, Bootstrap 3, JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX, PHP, MySQL
